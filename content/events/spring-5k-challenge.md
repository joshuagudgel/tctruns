## About This Race

Join us for our signature spring race through the beautiful Riverside Park. Perfect for runners of all levels with a flat, fast course that's ideal for setting personal records or completing your first 5K.

This race marks the beginning of our racing season and attracts runners from across the region for its scenic route and welcoming atmosphere.

## Course Information

### Course Details

- **Distance:** Certified 5K (3.1 miles)
- **Surface:** 100% paved park roads throughout
- **Elevation:** Minimal elevation change, net flat
- **Route:** Single loop course starting and ending at Riverside Park
- **Timing:** ChronoTrack chip timing for accurate results

### Course Markers

- Mile markers at 1, 2, and 3.1
- Water station at mile 1.5
- Finish line celebration area at Riverside Park pavilion
