## About This Race

Our festive holiday race through snow-covered trails. Costumes encouraged for this fun, community-focused event that celebrates the winter season!

This annual tradition brings families and runners together for a joyful conclusion to our racing year. It's more about fun and community than competition, though we still time everyone!

## Course Information

### Course Details

- **Distance:** 5K loop through Snow Valley Park trail system
- **Surface:** Mix of packed snow trails and plowed park roads
- **Terrain:** Gentle rolling hills through winter woods
- **Special feature:** Hot chocolate station at mile 2!
- **Conditions:** Varies based on snowfall and temperature

### Winter Course Notes

- **Weather dependent** - course may be modified for safety
- **Traction aids recommended** if icy conditions
- **Multiple loops possible** if snow limits trail access
- **Heated facilities** available at start/finish area

### What Makes This Special

- **Costume contest** with prizes for most creative winter outfits
- **Holiday decorations** throughout the course
- **Volunteer stations** with holiday cheer and encouragement
- **Post-race festivities** with community celebration

## Registration Details

### Holiday Fun Features

- **Costume contest** with prizes for most creative

- **Post-race hot chocolate and cookies** from local bakeries

## Weather & Preparation

### Dress for Success

- **Layer up!** Winter temperatures can vary greatly
- **Traction aids recommended** if icy (microspikes, yaktrax)
- **Waterproof shoes** if wet snow conditions
- **Gloves and hat** essential for comfort

### Weather Policy

- **Race held rain, snow, or shine** - we love winter weather!
- **Cancelled only for extreme conditions** (ice storms, blizzards)
- **Course modifications** possible based on conditions
- **Check our social media** morning of for any updates

### Winter Safety Tips

- Arrive extra early for potential weather delays
- Bring extra clothes for after the race
- Stay hydrated even in cold weather
- Watch for icy patches on course

## Directions & Parking

### Event Location

**Snow Valley Park**  
Winter Recreation Area, North Trail Access

### Parking Information

- **Free parking** at Snow Valley Park main lot
- **Winter road conditions** - allow extra travel time
- **4WD/AWD recommended** if recent snow
- **Carpooling encouraged** - parking may be limited by snow

### Getting There

- **From downtown:** Take Mountain Highway north for 20 minutes
- **Turn right** at Snow Valley Park sign
- **Follow winter access road** to recreation area
- **Park in designated areas** cleared by park maintenance
